CAS (Creativity, Activity, and Service)


In the time period of between 24 November to 24 December, me and my team spread the
message all over the school campus that we are initiating such a project where we will collect
the old clothes from everyone which is not useful anymore, so that we can distribute it to the poor
people who live in streets and does not have any clothes to protect themselves from the cold.
After collecting the clothes we then set the clothes in order so that it is easy to identify the clothes
which are for children, adults and old people. After sorting the clothes in order we then distributed it
to the poor people by searching for them on railways, streets, corners etc.

To spread awareness we created a YouTube Video with the title "Hate Poverty, not the Poor"
where we focused on few Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) too.
